For this project, I analyzed Netflix US daily top 10 during the COVID-19 pandemic. The decision to work on this project was  driven by curiosity to find out the preferences of Netflix subscribers in America during the COVID-19 pandemic. 

The time frame of the data is from April 1st, 2020 to March 11th, 2022. The dataset is a public dataset made available through Prasert Kanawattanachai on Kaggle. 

**Here is what I wanted to find out:**

1) Top 10 titles

2) Number of days for each category

3) Number of titles in each category

4) Number of Netflix productions vs. other productions

5) Number of days, the months and the years All American made the daily top 10 (Out of curiosity because it's one of my favourite TV shows)

## Findings

**Top 10 Titles**
<img width="1080" height="1009" alt="top 10 titles" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/35c79f7a-ffbb-4175-b85f-9191914ba8ef" />
Cocomelon was the most watched title; it was on the daily top 10 for 428 days. The other titles on the top 10 chart are Ozark (85 days), Cobra Kai (81 days), Manifest (80 days), The Queen's Gambit (73 days), Outer Banks (72 days), Squid Game (66 days), Bridgerton (58 days), All American (58 days), and Lucifer (56 days).

**Number of Days for Each Category**
<img width="1080" height="1000" alt="number of days for each category" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3bd25f5c-6525-4a5d-9083-9d4432a44a00" />
TV shows had the highest number of days, they appeared for 4,446 days, followed by movies which had 2,611 days, stand-up comedy and concert/performance had the least number of days with 41 and 2 days respectively.

**Number of Titles in Each Category**
<img width="1080" height="1009" alt="number of titles in each category" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fb3c41db-91c2-4562-8d1e-d0ac027835b4" />
354 of the titles were movies, followed by TV shows which had 280 titles, stand-up comedy and concert/performance were the least watched categories with 6 and 1 title(s) respectively.

**Netflix Productions vs. Other Productions**
<img width="1080" height="1004" alt="netflix vs  others" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/89676644-cb8b-419a-8472-1bd8571d3090" />
The number of titles produced by Netflix is 406 while other companies produced a total of 235 titles.

**All American**
<img width="1076" height="1008" alt="all american" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/856e1f30-91e6-4cd7-ba6c-e718144d6901" />
All American was one of the top 10 titles. It was on the daily top 10 for 58 days; one month in 2020 and three months in 2021. In April 2020, it was on the daily top 10 for 20 days. After April 2020, it appeared again on the daily top 10 on the last 4 days in July 2021, every day in August 2021 and the first three days in September 2021. I noticed this trend while exploring the dataset.
